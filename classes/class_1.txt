Class: Class_size

# Attributes

num_of_students(integer)
num_of_classrooms(integer)
num_of_seats(integer)
num_of_teachers(integer)


# Methods

calc_students_per_class(this divides all the num_of_students by num_of_classrooms to assign how many kids to each classroom)
calc_teach_staff_needed(this uses num_of_teachers and num_of_classrooms and num_of_students to calculate how many teachers they need to suffice the students and classrooms)
calc_chairs_needed(this will use the calc_students_per_class method to display the total amount of seats for a classroom)
calc_lunch_periods(this will use the num_of_students and the buildings cafeteria capacity to figure out how many lunch periods are needed)
